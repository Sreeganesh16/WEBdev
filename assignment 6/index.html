The history of coffee dates back to the 15th century, and possibly earlier with a number of reports and legends supporting its first use. The native (undomesticated) origin of coffee been is thought to have been Ethopia.

The earliest substantiated evidence of either coffee drinking or knowledge of the coffee tree is from the early 15th century , in the Sufi monasteries of Yemen, spreading soon to Mecca and Cairo.

cof.fee . 'ko-fe /

1.A beverage made by percolation ,infusion or decotion from the roasted and the ground seeds of a coffee plant.

2.any of the several old world tropical plants(genus Coffea and especially C. arabica and C. canephora) of the madder family that are widely cultivated in warm regions for their seeds from which coffee is prepared.
